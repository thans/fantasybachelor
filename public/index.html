    How It Works

    What is Fantasy Bachelorette?

    FantasyBach.com is a site intended to bring the fun of fantasy football to reality television. Your "team" will consist of up to 6 women that you think have the potential to be the last man standing in ABC's "The Bachelorette". You can select your team at any point during the week leading up to the show, but selections will close as soon as the show first airs - typically Mondays at 8PM EST.

    Pick Your Team

    Picking your team is simple. Go to the current week page by selecting it the menu in the top left corner of the screen. Then pick a contestant from the list of remaining contestants. You can then read their bio and click "Choose Me" if you want to choose them. You can choose 6 contestants per week (until the end of the season when there are less than 6 remaining contestants). If you realize you did not want to choose a contestant you can go back, click them again and then click "Remove Me"

    Scoring

    Scoring is simple. If a contestant you select gets a rose, you will get 1 point. If you pick that same contestant in the following week, you will get bonus points for them. Picking someone two weeks in a row makes them worth 1 point the first week and 2 points the second week, and so on. So pick the winner early, and you will earn lots of points!

    Your score will be available after the show airs on the west coast - usually 10PM PST. You can also check out the leaderboard (accessible from the menu in the top left corner of the screen) to see how you compare to other players. Leagues are not currently available, but look for that feature in future seasons of the show.
